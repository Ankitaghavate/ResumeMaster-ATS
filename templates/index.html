How to Build a Winning Resume

1
ATS-Friendly Layout

Use a single-column layout. Avoid images or tables inside your experience section as AI scanners often skip them.

2
Keyword Optimization

Identify hard skills from the job post (e.g., Python, Sales) and integrate them naturally into your bullet points.

3
Quantified Impact

Start every bullet with an action verb (e.g., 'Developed') and include measurable numbers (%, $).
